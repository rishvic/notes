[#buried-verbs]
== Buried verbs (zombie nouns)

A phrase that stacks abstract nouns ending in `-tion`, `-sion`, `-ment`, `-ance`
reads flat and hides who does what. Each such noun is usually a verb in
disguise: *decision* ← decide, *action* ← act, *analysis* ← analyse.

*Fix:* recover the buried verb and name who acts.

[cols="1,1",options="header"]
|===
| Flat (noun-heavy) | Direct (verb recovered)
| "what decisions need action" | "decide what to do" / "decide which to act on"
| "we conduct an analysis of the data" | "we analyse the data"
| "make a decision" | "decide"
| "take action on" | "act on"
|===
